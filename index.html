ABOUT US!
PLAYGROUND is a Dandy's World, Forsaken and Toon Universe inspired roblox game by @TimbitSilly!

The game follows a small town that has been closed off from the rest of the world for being infected with "Shrooms" with all adults dying and getting infected in the process. One school has somehow managed to stay uninfected.
